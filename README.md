## 📖 Overview
**QR Master Pro** is a modern, fast, and feature-rich Progressive Web Application (PWA) designed to handle all your QR code and barcode needs right from your browser. Whether you need to quickly scan a restaurant menu, read a product barcode, scan a saved image from your gallery, or generate your own custom codes, QR Master Pro does it all seamlessly without the need to install a bulky native app.

## 🎯 Purpose
The primary purpose of QR Master Pro is to provide an accessible, all-in-one utility for handling 2D and 1D codes. It bridges the gap between native mobile applications and web tools by offering a native app-like experience directly in the browser, combining both scanning and creation capabilities into a single lightweight platform.

## ✨ Key Features
- **Real-Time Camera Scanning**: Instantly scan QR codes and barcodes using your device's camera.
- **Gallery Upload**: Select and scan codes directly from images stored on your device.
- **Flashlight Support**: Built-in toggle to turn on your device's flashlight for scanning in low-light environments.
- **Scan History**: Keep track of your scanned items during your session, with quick actions to copy text or open URLs.
- **Code Generator**: Create custom QR codes and Code128 barcodes instantly by typing in text, URLs, or numbers.
- **Download Capability**: Save your newly generated QR codes and barcodes directly to your device.
- **Progressive Web App (PWA)**: Installable on your home screen for quick access and an immersive, full-screen experience.

## 🚀 Benefits
- **Zero Installation Required**: Runs entirely in your web browser. Just bookmark the link or add it to your home screen.
- **Saves Device Storage**: No need to download a heavy application just to scan a code.
- **Privacy First**: All scanning and generation algorithms run locally in your browser. Your camera feed and images are **never** uploaded to any external server.
- **Cross-Platform**: Works smoothly on iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux.
- **Beautiful UI/UX**: Designed with a clean, modern interface powered by Tailwind CSS to feel exactly like a premium mobile app.

## 🏆 Why is QR Master Pro Better Than Others?
1. **Ad-Free Experience**: Unlike many free scanner apps on the app stores that bombard you with full-screen ads, QR Master Pro is clean, fast, and completely free of interruptions.
2. **All-in-One Solution**: Most competitors either only scan *or* only generate. QR Master do both, and includes support for traditional barcodes as well.
3. **Advanced Web Features**: Capabilities like flashlight control and scanning from local image files are rarely found together in web-based scanners.
4. **Instant Access**: No waiting for app store downloads or updates. Just click the link and you're ready to scan in milliseconds.

## 🛠️ Technology Stack
- **Frontend Core**: HTML5, Vanilla JavaScript
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Icons**: Lucide Icons
- **Libraries used**: 
  - `html5-qrcode` (For reliable camera and file scanning)
  - `qrcodejs` (For QR Code generation)
  - `JsBarcode` (For Barcode generation)
